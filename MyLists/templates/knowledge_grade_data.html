Knowledge grade data

    All these grades are taken from the unity assets store.
    There are 24 different grades.
    You gain a grade each 15 levels.
    The knowledge grades are based on the sum of the differents levels (Series, Anime, Books).
    We use the linear equation: grade = (lvl(Series)+lvl(Anime)+lvl(Books))/15.
