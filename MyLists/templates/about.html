About Mylists.info

We are only 2 french persons to maintain this website. We are amateurs and do not pretends to be good programmers (nor to speak good english). It is just a small project on our free time to know where we were in our series/anime or movies for us and our friends. If you have any constructive remarks, find any bugs or want to be parts of the evolution of Mylists.info do not hesitate and contact us .
