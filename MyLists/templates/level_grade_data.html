Levels and grade data

    All these grades are taken from the video game Halo 3.
    There are 42 different grades and 125 levels.
    You gain a grade each 3 levels.
    The levels are based on the time spent on each category (Series, Anime, Books).
    We use the quadratic equation: Level = 50*XP*(1+XP).
