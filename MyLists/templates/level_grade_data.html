Levels and grade data

    All these grades are taken from the video game Halo Reach.
    There are 49 different grades and 147 levels.
    You gain a grade each 3 levels.
    The levels are based on the time spent on each category (Series, Anime, Movies).
    We use the quadratic equation: XP = 20*lvl*(1+lvl) <==> Level = (sqrt(400+80*XP)-20)/40
    1min = 1XP taken from the series and anime episode duration, and from the runtime for the movies.
