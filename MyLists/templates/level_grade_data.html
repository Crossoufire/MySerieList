Levels and Grade data

    All these Grades are taken from the video game Halo 3.
    There are 42 different Grades and 125 levels.
    You gain a Grade each 3 levels.
    The levels are based on the time spent on each category (Series, Animes, Books).
    We use the quadratic equation: XP = 25*lvl*(1+lvl) <==> Level = (sqrt(625+100*XP)-25)/50
    1min = 1XP taken from the episode duration for series and anime and 1page = 1XP for the books.
